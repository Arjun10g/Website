Current Work

Active Research

SEM Psychometrics
Influential Functions in Structural Equation Modelling

Developing influence diagnostics for SEM using influence function theory — identifying observations that disproportionately affect parameter estimates, model fit indices, and path coefficients in latent variable models.

Statistics Non-Parametric
The SaddlePoint Divergence Metric (SPDM)

A non-parametric probability density-based framework for quantifying distributional differences — leveraging saddlepoint approximations to construct a novel divergence metric with strong theoretical guarantees. Manuscript under review.

Under Review · Communications in Statistics – Simulation and Computation
SEM Optimisation
Gradient-Based Trajectory Informed Optimiser for SEM

Adapting gradient-based trajectory optimisation methods to structural equation modelling — replacing traditional ML/GLS estimators with trajectory-informed gradient descent to improve convergence in complex, high-dimensional SEM specifications.

Previous Work

Past Projects

NLP Deep Learning
Twitter Sentiment & Topic Modelling at Scale

Scraped and preprocessed 500k+ tweets. Fine-tuned BERT and RoBERTa for sentiment classification; applied BERTopic for unsupervised topic discovery. Full PyTorch pipeline with mixed-precision training and custom evaluation.

Statistics Mental Health
Mental Health Trajectories — Syrian Refugees in Canada

Applied LCGA to model longitudinal mental health trajectories. Built automated Python pipelines for survey data cleaning and reproducible reporting in collaboration with the University of Dubai.

Full-Stack FastAPI
Linguistics Web Applications (York University)

Built FastAPI + React tools for the Linguistics department. Developed data pipelines for phonetic and corpus data processing, enabling researchers to run analyses without programming expertise.

Statistics Simulation
Regularisation in Mediation Models — Monte Carlo Study

Large-scale Monte Carlo simulation comparing Lasso, Ridge, and Elastic Net penalties in multiple mediation models. Evaluated estimation bias and Type I error rates across varying sample sizes and correlation structures.

MLM Longitudinal
Student Transition Study — High School to University Chemistry

Logistic regression and factor analysis investigating predictors of first-year university chemistry success. Produced visualisations and policy-relevant reports for York University's Associate Dean.

Youth Mixed Methods
Adolescent Treatment Trajectories — Pine River Institute

Synthesised longitudinal adolescent treatment data. Mixed-methods analysis of outcomes before, during, and after COVID-19; created best-practice statistical guidelines for the institute.
